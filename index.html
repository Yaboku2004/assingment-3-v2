Overview

+ Topic:

Create open-world adventure games with engaging stories, immersive gameplay, and detailed environments with heritage and realistic sightseeing worldwide. Players can explore vast landscapes, interact with NPCs, and discover all the heritage and sightseeing worldwide. It offers exploring options like planes, ships, cars, and motorbikes. It presents challenges requiring strategy, knowledge, and skill. Ultimately it creates a satisfying experience for the player.

Our wanted outcomes are to receive investment and popularity from the public and receive their support. The next thing is that when we make video games, we can be exposed to many other fields like graphic design, advanced programming, or collaborating with famous companies such as “Mihoyo” and “Mojang”. Lastly, we want to create something that can entertain people and partly combine with learning to help improve knowledge or increase creativity and intelligence for young people instead of just studying at school. We also want everyone to know about other countries' history and beautiful sights.

+ Motivation:

Several reasons motivated me to develop this game. First and foremost, we are huge fans of the open world, exploration, adventure, and history, and combining these genres creates an exciting and unique experience. Next, I would like to examine the concepts of freedom, learning, and hope in the context of video games. These are important and relevant issues today, and video games can be a powerful tool to address them. Thirdly, this game will be a great addition to my portfolio and show potential employers that I can create new and exciting video games. One of the significant trends in IT today is the development of video games, a popular form of entertainment that brings tons of revenue to the entertainment industry. New video game technologies, such as virtual and augmented reality, also bring new possibilities to the industry.

+ Landscape:

There are many similar video games out there today, such as “Genshin Impact”, “Honkai Impact”, and “Minecraft”,... However, I believe my game will be unique as it blends aspects of open-world games. I also think that the story and ideas of my project will be different from other video games on the market. My video game differs from existing video games in several ways. First, it merges exploration, learning, and solving. This provides players with a unique and immersive experience. Second, my games have in-depth stories about the country’s freedom, history, learning, and knowledge. These are essential and timely issues in today's world, and I believe video games can be an effective way to explore them. Third, my game would look great. We intend to build an immersive and realistic world by combining cutting-edge video game technology.

Scope and Limits

In this project, the most challenging thing to do is multiplayer mode. Another limitation is tracing the world in every detail. This section will require much time to develop and fix the error. In the first step, we will launch the game in Single Player Mode, where there is only one user in the world, and they will be surrounded by NPC (Non-playable character). In the game's early access, players can only enjoy a part of the world because the game is still developing. However, the mechanism and systems that have been completed exclude the multiplayer mode.

Tools and Technologies

We use Unity3D to develop the game's environment, obstacles, and material. The programming language which we will use is C Sharp (CS) Blender is also used to create players' models. We need to have a Unity3D and Blender license to make the game. The recommended requirement for hardware is: Unity3D: 16GB RAM, 64-bit eight-core CPU, GPU with more than 4GB VRAM, Full HD Monitor, mouse, trackpad, or tablet+pen. Blender: 16GB RAM, 64-bit eight-core CPU, GPU with more than 4GB VRAM, Full HD Monitor, mouse, trackpad, or tablet+pen. The version for Unity3D is 2021.3, and Blender is 3.5.

Testing

In the testing process, we will test each part differently. For example, if we want to determine whether the game operates correctly, we will launch a test server allowing players to participate. Network testing for multiplayer will be tested when the game is about to launch the multiplayer mode. When there is no bug or error feedback from our users, we will know if our game has run smoothly, and we will proceed to another step and continue to test. The tester will need to pre-register to try the game. There will be a limited sign-up for testing. We randomly pick who will become a tester; no background is required. The estimated number of testers will be around 2000 users. During the testing time, players will be able to send us feedback about bugs and errors. Even when the game is complete, they can do it.
